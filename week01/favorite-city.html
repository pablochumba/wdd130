Paris

Paris is basically that city everyone already knows, even if they’ve never been there: romantic almost by default, a little chaotic, full of history, and with a vibe that feels like an old movie but in HD.

You’ve got the Eiffel Tower popping up everywhere, small streets that look straight out of a postcard, and cafés where people sit for hours just talking or pretending to be deep. Everything looks so elegant it low-key makes you walk better without realizing it..
